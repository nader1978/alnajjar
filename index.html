What Do We Do?

NN Associates is one of the leading accounting companies in Dubai that offers robust, reliable, and efficient accounting services across the UAE. We are a team of qualified professionals having years of experience with Big 4 and leading auditing firms in Dubai. We are well-versed with the accounting regulations in UAE and expert in IFRS and international accounting standards as well.

As per the UAE Commercial Company Law 2015 and UAE VAT Law, every registered company in the country is entitled to maintain the books of account. At NN, we offer customized solutions taking into account the unique nature of your business without having to set up a full-fledged finance department in your company in Dubai. Our expert team takes care of the legal and technical aspects of accounting so that you can focus on other areas of business operations. The core strength of NN lies within the adept team who is highly proficient, well trained & updated with recent changes in IFRS. Our well thought out accounting & auditing practices can establish proper financial discipline in your organization.
